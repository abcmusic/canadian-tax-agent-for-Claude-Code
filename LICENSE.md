## Disclaimer

This software provides tax preparation guidance based on Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) published rules and regulations. While the system aims for accuracy:

1. **No Professional Advice:** This software does not constitute professional tax, legal, or financial advice. Consult a qualified Canadian tax professional (CPA, chartered accountant) for complex situations.

2. **Accuracy:** Tax rules are complex and subject to interpretation. Users are responsible for reviewing all calculations and forms before submission to CRA.

3. **CRA Compliance:** Users must verify that all completed forms comply with current CRA requirements before filing.

4. **No Liability:** The authors and contributors accept no liability for any errors, omissions, or tax liabilities arising from use of this software.

5. **Data Privacy:** While this software encrypts sensitive data and processes everything locally, users are responsible for securing their own data.

6. **Updates:** Tax rules change annually. Users must update the system regularly to ensure current CRA rules are applied.

7. **Professional Review Recommended:** For non-trivial tax situations, professional review of completed returns is strongly recommended before filing.
